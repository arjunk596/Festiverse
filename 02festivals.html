Diwali

Celebrated as the festival of lights, Diwali symbolizes the victory of light over darkness.

Holi

Holi is a vibrant festival of colors celebrated to mark the victory of good over evil.

Navratri

Navratri celebrates the triumph of Goddess Durga over the buffalo demon Mahishasura.

Ganesh Chaturthi

Ganesh Chaturthi honors Lord Ganesha, the god of beginnings and knowledge.

Eid-ul-Fitr

Eid-ul-Fitr marks the end of Ramadan and is celebrated with feasts and prayers.

Christmas

Christmas celebrates the birth of Jesus Christ and is marked with gifts and family gatherings.

Pongal

Pongal is a harvest festival celebrated in Tamil Nadu to thank the Sun God for a bountiful harvest.

Raksha Bandhan

Raksha Bandhan celebrates the bond between brothers and sisters with a sacred thread.
